our story

how we met

from H

In late 2017, Jenn and I were both (separately) moving to the Bay Area to start our new jobs. We found each other through a dating app and connected over our shared interest for the outdoors and connection to the Pacific Northwest (my childhood home). We decided to meet up over coffee + hike on a breezy January day. While the view was less than spectacular that day due to cloudy conditions, I was able to learn so much about her, including the fact that she can hike faster than me! I loved how caring and down-to-earth she was and love the fact that I could make her laugh with my corny jokes. After a few more dates we made it official. Jenn and I have created lots of memories with each other over the past three years and we can't wait to make much more, as husband and wife.

from J

I was working in Sacramento at the time after grad school and had set my Coffee Meets Bagel app location to SF since I had just accepted a new job offer back in the Bay. I had tapped no to everyone that had popped up -- until one day on my lunch break Hien’s profile showed up and I thought oh no.. he actually looks like a nice guy and I can’t think of any excuses to tap no. So I left it for a full 24 hours and after lots of encouragement from my coworkers, went back and double tapped the heart by his name. We exchanged numbers and planned to meet up after the new year. Weeks went by, so when he texted me again I was surprised he remembered! Little did I know then, that he would be my first and last date through the app. Our first date turned into a coffee + two hikes + late lunch hangout over 7 hours long. A few days later, he surprised me after work with a trip to the ice cream shop by my place. Within two weeks, we had planned our first snowboarding trip to Tahoe and booked our first campsite at Yosemite. From then on he has become my very best friend, and I can't wait to marry him.

plan your trip

flights

There are no direct flights to Hood River.
Hood River is ~ 1 hour drive from Portland International Airport (PDX).

accommodations

The Orchard is 5-7 min from most Hood River Hotels. Most local hotels do not offer room blocks but offer cancellations without a fee.

getting to The Orchard

There are plenty of on-site parking spots available, but we encourage you to carpool with those you are traveling with. All cars should be removed by the end of the night. Cars that have not been picked up by 9 am the next day will be towed.

There is no reliable taxi service or ride sharing in Hood River.

COVID precautions

Hood River County is now permanently in the Low Risk Category. Our venue is entirely outdoors and masks are not required. Social distancing is not required for fully vaccinated guests. The venue will not be checking your vaccination status. We recommend bringing a mask with you regardless of your vaccination status in case you need it.

venue rules

Please note guests are not allowed to arrive before 3:00 pm and must leave the venue by 10:00 pm.

Pets are not allowed.
