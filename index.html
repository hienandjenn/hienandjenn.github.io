getting to The Orchard

There are 67 on-site parking spots. All cars should be removed by the end of the night. However we do not want guests to drink and drive, and if needed, cars can be left until 8 am the following morning. Cars that have not been picked up by 9 am will be towed.

There is no reliable taxi service or ride sharing in Hood River. There are some taxis, but they are not always reliable or available, so we would not recommend you plan to depend on them to get home.

covid precautions

Our venue is well-informed about social distancing measures and gathering restrictions that are part of Oregon COVID compliance for gatherings. Currently, they will not seat more than 10 people (of the same exposure group) at each table, and tables must be spaced 6 feet apart. Weddings are required two cleaners and one social distance monitor per 50 guests. Please check back as we will update if anything changes.

venue rules

Please note guests are not allowed to arrive before 3:00 pm and must leave the venue by 10:00 pm.

Pets are not allowed.

Please kindly adhere to the above rules to help us avoid being charged penalty fees!
